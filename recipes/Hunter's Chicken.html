Hunter's Chicken

About Hunters Chicken

What could be more comforting than chicken breasts wrapped in bacon with BBQ sauce and cheese? Serve this easy chicken dish with mash or roast potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil, plus extra for the tray
  • 2 skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 rashers smoked back bacon
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • Â½ pack runner beans, ends trimmed
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 50ml sticky BBQ sauce
  • 25g grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6
  2. While the chicken is cooking, fry the onion in 1 tbsp oil until golden brown and soft, then set aside.
  3. Once the chicken is cooked, pour over the BBQ sauce and scatter over the parmesan and return to the oven for 2 mins until the cheese has melted. Serve with the mash, carrots and runner beans.
  4. Cool & Serve
